About our metrics
The metrics presented for each property are constructed heuristically based upon the fraction of a property coverged by the tree canopy.

For heating/cooling costs, we use the regional heating/cooling degree days from the EIA and assume that 2/3rds of all days of the year will require either heating or cooling. A full canopy of trees is then assumed to affect a 5° change on these days.

For noise abatement, a complete coverage of a property by trees is assumed to effect a 10 dB reduction in ambient noise, this is reduced if coverage is less than total.

Lastly, we assume that a property with complete tree coverage will have a 10% increase in home value (pulled from Zillow), with smaller coverage fractions corresponding to a proportionate decrease.
